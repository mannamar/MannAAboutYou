Humble Beginnings

Hello world! My name is Amar. I was born in Flint, MI- #3 on Forbe's inaugural list of The Most Miserable Cities In America. My parents wanting nothing but the best for us soon moved to Stockton, CA which ranks #2 on that same list. Jokes aside growing up in Stockton was great. Everyone in my family lives there so I grew up very close to my grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, second cousins, great aunts, great uncles, etc.

I spent most of my adult years in the Bay Area. It's a great place to live with awesome people, delicious food, and random events pretty much every weekend. In college I studied Biology but eventually realized that neither healthcare nor labwork were my thing. I'm currently back home in Stockton, a student at Codestack Academy, and hopefully on my way to becoming a future software engineer.
